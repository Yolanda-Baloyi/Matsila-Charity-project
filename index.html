MatricDance

First Ever Matric Dance Of Radzambo Secondary School

The school serves the disadvantaged community of Matsila Village, in the Limpopo province. The school was established in 1987 and never had a Matric Dance for the students. Class of 2013 was given a special send-off into the world on the 12th October 2013. Matsila Youth Development Forum raised money to provide an unforgettable evening to the 15 Matric students as well as their parents at a nearby lodge (Joe Matsila Lodge). For more pictures, please visit our facebook page.

Radzambo Secondary School

Radzambo Secondary School (EMIS NO: 930321578)

The school was established in 1987 and only Grade 8 to 10. Grade 11 and 12 were introduced in 1995. The building as seen below was build in 1986 and has only 5 classrooms. Since then, it was never renovated and is totally dilapilated. The school still does not offer physical science and commercial subjects. For more pictures, please visit our facebook page.

Sanitation for All 2014

Sanitation for All 2014

The campaign was about raising funds to renovate the toilets of Radzambo Secondary School. The toilets were build in 1986 and were totally dilapidated. Our goal was to improve the lives of the toilet-less students. The campaign made it possible to restore the toilets to its original state. Thanks to all who contributed towards this initiative. For more pictures, please visit our facebook page.
